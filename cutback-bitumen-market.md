<p><p>Cutback bitumen is categorized into three main types based on the rate of curing: Rapid Curing (RC), Medium Curing (MC), and Slow Curing (SC). RC is designed for quick evaporation, making it suitable for cold weather applications and immediate use. MC offers a balanced evaporation rate, ideal for moderate climates and construction processes. SC, with a slower evaporation rate, is used in hot climates or areas requiring extended setting times. These classifications help optimize performance in various environmental conditions and applications.</p></p>
