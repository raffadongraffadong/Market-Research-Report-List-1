<p><p>The High Content Screening (HCS) Station market can be categorized primarily into Brightfield and Fluorescence imaging techniques. Brightfield imaging utilizes visible light to assess cellular and tissue samples, providing insights into sample morphology and structure. In contrast, Fluorescence imaging leverages fluorescent dyes or proteins to visualize specific cellular components, allowing for more detailed analysis of biological processes and interactions. Both techniques are essential in drug discovery, toxicology, and biology, enabling researchers to obtain quantitative data from complex cellular environments.</p></p>
