<p><p>A Variable Shunt Reactor (VSR) is an essential device used in electrical systems to manage reactive power and stabilize voltage levels. Unlike fixed reactors, VSRs can adjust their impedance to adapt to varying conditions, making them ideal for improving power quality in transmission and distribution networks.
