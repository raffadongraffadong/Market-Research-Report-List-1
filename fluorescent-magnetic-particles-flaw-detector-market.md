<p><p>The Fluorescent Magnetic Particles Flaw Detector market comprises two primary types: magnetic yokes and magnetic benches. Magnetic yokes are portable devices used for field inspection, employing an electromagnet to generate a magnetic field, allowing for the detection of surface and slightly subsurface defects in ferromagnetic materials. Meanwhile, magnetic benches are stationary systems designed for more extensive inspections, providing a robust platform for large components. Both types utilize fluorescent magnetic particles to enhance flaw visibility, ensuring effective quality control in manufacturing and maintenance processes.</p></p>
