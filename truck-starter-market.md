<p><strong>The Truck Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck starter market comprises two primary types: direct control and electromagnetic control. Direct control starters utilize a mechanical linkage that activates the starter motor directly, offering reliability and straightforward functionality. In contrast, electromagnetic control starters operate via an electromagnetic switch, allowing for remote activation and improved efficiency. Both types cater to different needs, with direct control favored for simplicity and durability, while electromagnetic control is preferred for its enhanced control and integration with modern electrical systems.</p></p>
