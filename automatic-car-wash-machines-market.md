<p><strong>The Automatic Car Wash Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gantry Car Wash</li><li>Tunnel Car Wash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic car wash machines market includes two primary types: gantry car washes and tunnel car washes. Gantry car washes feature a stationary structure that moves over the vehicle, using fixed washing equipment and providing a thorough clean. In contrast, tunnel car washes involve a conveyor system that pulls vehicles through a lengthy wash tunnel equipped with various cleaning machines and drying systems. Both types offer efficient and automated washing solutions, catering to different consumer preferences and operational scales.</p></p>
