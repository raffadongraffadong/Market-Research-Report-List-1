<p><ul><li>Switch Controller</li><li>Proportional Controller</li><li>Pid Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The panel mount temperature controller market encompasses several types: switch controllers, proportional controllers, and PID controllers. Switch controllers simply turn heating or cooling devices on or off based on a set temperature threshold. Proportional controllers adjust the output proportionally to the difference from the set point, offering smoother control. PID controllers combine proportional, integral, and derivative actions to minimize error over time, providing precise and stable temperature regulation. Each type serves different application needs, emphasizing control precision and response time.</p></p>
