<p><p>DVT screening and treatment devices encompass various market types aimed at diagnosing and managing deep vein thrombosis. Imaging modalities include ultrasound and MRI for visualizing venous clots. Intravascular ultrasound provides detailed internal views of veins. Venous stenting involves placing a device to keep veins open, improving blood flow. Compression stockings are used to prevent clot formation and enhance venous return. Other devices may include anticoagulant delivery systems and peripheral vascular interventions, further aiding in DVT management.</p></p>
