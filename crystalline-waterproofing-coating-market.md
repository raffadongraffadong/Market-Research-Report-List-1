<p><strong>The Crystalline Waterproofing Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cement-based Permeable Crystalline Waterproof Coating</li><li>Water-based Permeable Crystalline Waterproof Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crystalline waterproofing coating market includes two primary types: cement-based permeable crystalline waterproof coating and water-based permeable crystalline waterproof coating. </p><p>Cement-based permeable coatings utilize cement as a binder, incorporating crystalline technology to form a waterproof barrier by sealing pores and micro-cracks in concrete. Water-based coatings, on the other hand, are formulated using water as a solvent, allowing for easier application and faster drying times, while still offering protective properties and flexibility in various environmental conditions. Both types enhance durability and longevity of structures.</p></p>
