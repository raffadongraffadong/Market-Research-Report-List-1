Limb splints cater to fractures or soft tissue injuries in arms and legs, providing immobilization and ease of movement. Torso splints support the chest and abdomen, often used for rib fractures or spinal injuries, promoting healing while minimizing discomfort. Together, these products enhance patient care and recovery in various medical scenarios.</p></p>
