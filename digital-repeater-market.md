<p><p>The digital repeater market is segmented based on the number of frequency bands they support. Quad-band digital repeaters enhance signals across four frequency bands, making them ideal for diverse applications in urban areas. Tri-band digital repeaters operate over three bands, suitable for medium-range coverage. Dual-band digital repeaters serve two frequencies, often used in specific industries or regions. Single-band digital repeaters focus on one frequency, typically utilized in niche markets or rural areas, providing targeted signal amplification.</p></p>
