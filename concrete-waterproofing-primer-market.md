<p><strong>The Concrete Waterproofing Primer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete waterproofing primer market is categorized into one-component and two-component systems. One-component primers are ready-to-use, requiring no mixing, which simplifies application and reduces labor time. They typically offer good adhesion and are ideal for less demanding environments. In contrast, two-component primers consist of separate resin and hardener components that, when mixed, provide superior performance and durability. They are often used in more challenging conditions, offering enhanced water resistance and longevity, making them suitable for critical applications in construction and infrastructure.</p></p>
