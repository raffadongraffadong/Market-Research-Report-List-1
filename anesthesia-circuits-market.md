<p><strong>The Anesthesia Circuits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-open Circuits</li><li>Open Circuits</li><li>Closed Circuits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anesthesia circuits market consists of three main types: semi-open, open, and closed circuits. Semi-open circuits allow for some gas exchange with the atmosphere while maintaining some rebreathing, facilitating controlled ventilation. Open circuits completely exhaust exhaled gases, offering minimal resistance but less efficiency. Closed circuits, on the other hand, completely recirculate gas, enhancing efficiency and minimizing waste. Each circuit type serves different clinical needs, balancing factors such as environmental safety, patient comfort, and anesthesia depth management.</p></p>
