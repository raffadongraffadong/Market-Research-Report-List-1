<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electronic Power Steering</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle steering systems market includes three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electronic Power Steering (EPS), and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). HPS uses hydraulic pressure to assist steering, offering reliability but lower fuel efficiency. EPS employs electric motors for assistance, providing better fuel economy and precision. EHPS combines hydraulic and electric systems, balancing efficiency and performance. Each system caters to different vehicle needs, with EPS gaining popularity due to its energy-saving benefits, while HPS and EHPS remain relevant for specific applications.</p></p>
