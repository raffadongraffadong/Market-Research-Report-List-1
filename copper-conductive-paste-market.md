<p><p>The Copper Conductive Paste market is categorized into three types based on sintering temperatures: Low Temperature Sintered, Medium Temperature Sintered, and High Temperature Sintered. Low Temperature Sintered pastes operate around 150-250°C, suitable for sensitive substrates. Medium Temperature Sintered pastes require 250-350°C, balancing performance and application versatility. High Temperature Sintered pastes function above 350°C, providing robust conductivity for demanding environments. Each type serves various industries, including electronics, automotive, and renewable energy, emphasizing the importance of temperature control in performance and reliability.</p></p>
