<p><strong>The Interchangeable Lens Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interchangeable lens cameras primarily utilize two types of image sensors: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their superior image quality and low noise levels, making them ideal for professional photography. However, they consume more power. Conversely, CMOS sensors are widely favored for their energy efficiency, faster processing speeds, and the ability to integrate additional functions on-chip. This versatility has led CMOS to dominate the interchangeable lens camera market in recent years.</p></p>
