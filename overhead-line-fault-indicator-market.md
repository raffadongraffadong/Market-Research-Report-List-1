<p><strong>The Overhead Line Fault Indicator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pole-mounted Indicator</li><li>Conductor-mounted Indicator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Overhead Line Fault Indicator market is primarily categorized into two types: pole-mounted indicators and conductor-mounted indicators. Pole-mounted indicators are installed on utility poles, providing real-time monitoring of fault conditions along overhead lines, enhancing maintenance efficiency and safety. In contrast, conductor-mounted indicators are attached directly to the power conductors, offering localized detection of faults and minimizing response times. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring reliable power distribution by promptly identifying issues in overhead electrical networks.</p></p>
