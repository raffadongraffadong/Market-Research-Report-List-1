<p><p>The 5G RAN (Radio Access Network) equipment market is segmented into two main types: 5G Non-Standalone (NSA) and 5G Standalone (SA) equipment. 5G NSA equipment integrates with existing 4G infrastructure to enhance data speeds and capacity, making it easier for operators to deploy 5G services quickly.
