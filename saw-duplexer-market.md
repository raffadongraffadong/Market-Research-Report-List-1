<p><strong>The SAW Duplexer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unbalanced Type</li><li>Balanced Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SAW duplexer market is divided into two main types: unbalanced and balanced. Unbalanced duplexers typically feature a single port for both transmit and receive signals, offering a simpler design and lower cost, making them suitable for consumer applications. In contrast, balanced duplexers have separate ports for transmit and receive, providing better isolation and performance, ideal for communication systems requiring high efficiency and minimal interference. Each type caters to different performance needs and market segments.</p></p>
