<p><p>The Automotive Firmware Over The Air (FOTA) market is primarily categorized into Cellular and Wi-Fi segments. Cellular FOTA employs mobile networks to deliver firmware updates, ensuring wide coverage and accessibility even in remote areas. In contrast, Wi-Fi FOTA utilizes local wireless networks, enabling faster data transfer rates and larger updates when vehicles are parked. Both methods enhance vehicle performance and security by allowing manufacturers to streamline updates, address issues promptly, and improve overall user experience while minimizing downtime.</p></p>
