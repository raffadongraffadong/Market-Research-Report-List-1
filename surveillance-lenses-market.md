<p><p>The surveillance lenses market comprises two primary types: auto iris and manual iris lenses. Auto iris lenses adjust the aperture automatically based on lighting conditions, providing optimal image clarity in varying environments. This feature is particularly beneficial for dynamic scenes. In contrast, manual iris lenses require users to adjust the aperture settings manually, offering greater control in stable lighting conditions but necessitating more user intervention. Both types serve distinct purposes in enhancing surveillance system performance and image quality.</p></p>
