<p><strong>The Connected Motorcycles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellular V2X</li><li>Dedicated Short Range Communication</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Connected Motorcycles market consists of two primary types of communication technologies: Cellular V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) and Dedicated Short Range Communication (DSRC). Cellular V2X utilizes cellular networks to enable real-time communication between motorcycles, vehicles, and infrastructure, enhancing safety, navigation, and traffic management. DSRC, on the other hand, is a short-range, high-speed wireless communication system specifically designed for automotive applications. It allows vehicles to exchange information quickly, improving situational awareness and reducing accident risks. Both technologies aim to enhance the overall riding experience and safety.</p></p>
