<p><p>The Fabry-Perot laser market is primarily divided into two types: single mode and broadband. Single mode lasers emit light at a single wavelength, providing high coherence and stable output, making them ideal for applications like telecommunications and precision measurements. In contrast, broadband lasers produce a wider range of wavelengths, offering greater versatility for applications requiring multiple frequencies, such as spectroscopy and optical sensing. Each type serves distinct market needs, catering to varying technological requirements and applications.</p></p>
