<p><p>The dental barrier membrane bone graft market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes are designed to gradually break down in the body, eliminating the need for a second surgery and promoting natural tissue regeneration. Non-absorbable membranes, on the other hand, remain in place long-term and may require surgical removal later. These products are vital in periodontal and implant surgeries, aiding in tissue regeneration and helping maintain bone structure during healing.</p></p>
