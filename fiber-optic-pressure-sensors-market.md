Wavelength Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors exhibit pressure variations through wavelength shifts of the transmitted light. </p><p>4. Polarization Modulated Fiber Optic Sensors assess pressure based on alterations in light polarization. Each type offers distinct advantages, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
