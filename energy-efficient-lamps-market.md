<p><p>The energy-efficient lamps market consists of several key types. Fluorescent lamps use gas and phosphor to produce light, offering great efficiency for general lighting. Induction lamps utilize electromagnetic fields to excite gas, providing long lifespans and low maintenance. High-Intensity Discharge (HID) lamps generate light through gas ionization, ideal for outdoor and industrial applications. Light Emitting Diode (LED) lamps are highly energy-efficient and versatile, rapidly gaining popularity. Other options may include compact fluorescent lamps and specialty lighting solutions, catering to specific needs.</p></p>
