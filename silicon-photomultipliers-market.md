<p><p>The Silicon Photomultipliers (SiPM) market is primarily divided into two types: Analog and Digital SiPMs. Analog SiPMs offer high sensitivity and linearity for detecting low light levels, making them suitable for applications in medical imaging and particle physics. In contrast, Digital SiPMs integrate signal processing capabilities within the device, enabling advanced functionalities like higher noise immunity and faster data readout. This differentiation allows manufacturers to cater to various sectors, including healthcare, automotive, and telecommunications, enhancing overall market growth.</p></p>
