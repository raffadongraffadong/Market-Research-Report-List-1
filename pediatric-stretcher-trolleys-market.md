<p><p>Pediatric stretcher trolleys are specialized transport devices designed for children in medical settings, categorized into four main types. Manual trolleys rely on human effort for adjustments, ensuring simplicity and ease of use. Hydraulic trolleys use a fluid mechanism for height and angle adjustments, allowing smooth transitions. Electric trolleys feature motorized controls for effortless operation and precise positioning. Pneumatic trolleys utilize air pressure for lifting and lowering, offering flexibility and enhanced maneuverability, catering to diverse pediatric needs in healthcare environments.</p></p>
