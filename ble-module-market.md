<p><strong>The BLE Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode BLE Modules</li><li>Dual-Mode BLE Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The BLE module market is categorized into two primary types: Single-Mode and Dual-Mode BLE modules. Single-Mode BLE modules are designed exclusively for Bluetooth Low Energy communications, making them ideal for power-sensitive applications that require minimal energy use. In contrast, Dual-Mode BLE modules support both Bluetooth Classic and Bluetooth Low Energy protocols, offering greater flexibility for various applications. This dual functionality is advantageous for devices needing support for legacy Bluetooth connections while also utilizing the energy-efficient benefits of BLE.</p></p>
