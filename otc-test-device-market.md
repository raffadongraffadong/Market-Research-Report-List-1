<p><p>The OTC test device market includes various types of diagnostic tests that enable rapid and convenient health assessments. Lateral flow assays are rapid tests that use capillary action to deliver results on a test strip, commonly used for pregnancy or infectious diseases. Immunoassays employ antigen-antibody interactions for specific biomarker detection. Dipstick tests involve a strip with reactive pads that change color based on the presence of certain substances, often used for urine analysis, offering quick diagnostic insight at home.</p></p>
