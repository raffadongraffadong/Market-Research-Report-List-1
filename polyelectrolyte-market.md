<p><ul><li>Polysalts</li><li>Polycations</li><li>Polyanions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **polyelectrolyte market** is segmented into **polysalts**, **polycations**, and **polyanions** based on their ionic properties. **Polysalts** are formed by the combination of oppositely charged polyelectrolytes, offering stability and versatility in applications like water treatment. **Polycations** carry positive charges, making them effective in flocculation, antimicrobial coatings, and gene delivery. **Polyanions** have negative charges and are widely used in superabsorbents, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals. Each type serves distinct roles in industries, driven by their unique charge-based interactions and functional properties.</p></p>
