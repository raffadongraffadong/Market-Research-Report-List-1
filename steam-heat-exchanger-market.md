<p><strong>The Steam Heat Exchanger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Heating Equipment</li><li>Indirect Heating Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Steam Heat Exchanger Market includes two primary types: direct heating equipment and indirect heating equipment. Direct heating equipment transfers steam directly to the substance being heated, ensuring quick heat transfer and efficiency in processes like cooking or sterilization. Indirect heating equipment, on the other hand, utilizes a heat transfer medium, such as water or oil, to convey heat from steam to the target substance, providing greater control over temperature and safety in applications like pasteurization and HVAC systems.</p></p>
