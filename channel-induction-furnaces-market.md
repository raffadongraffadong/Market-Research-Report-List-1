<p><p>Channel induction furnaces are categorized into two primary market types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal channel induction furnaces feature a horizontal design that facilitates easier loading and unloading of materials, making them ideal for large-volume production and continuous operation. In contrast, vertical channel induction furnaces have an upright configuration, which optimizes space and offers enhanced thermal efficiency, suitable for processes requiring precise temperature control. Both types serve diverse industrial applications including metal melting and refining.</p></p>
