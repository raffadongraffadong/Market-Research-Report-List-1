<p><p>Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) and Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) vehicles are alternative fuel vehicles that use CNG or LPG as their primary energy source instead of traditional gasoline or diesel. Both fuels are cleaner-burning alternatives, reducing greenhouse gas emissions and air pollutants compared to conventional fuels. CNG is commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles like buses and trucks, while LPG is popular in light-duty vehicles and passenger cars.</p><p>The Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) and Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) Vehicles Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing environmental concerns, government incentives, and the rising cost of conventional fuels. The Asia-Pacific region is leading the market due to high demand in countries like India and China, where governments are actively promoting cleaner fuels. Overall, the market is poised for robust growth as consumers and industries increasingly prioritize eco-friendly and cost-effective transportation options.</p></p>
