<p><p>Dried strawberry market types mainly include baked dried and freeze-dried strawberries. Baked dried strawberries are produced by dehydrating fresh strawberries through an oven process, resulting in a chewy texture and concentrated flavor, often used in snacks and baked goods. In contrast, freeze-dried strawberries undergo a process where moisture is removed at low temperatures, preserving their structure, color, and nutritional value. This method results in a lightweight, crunchy product, ideal for cereals, smoothies, and gourmet dishes.</p></p>
