<p><ul><li>Charcoal Hemoperfusion</li><li>Certain Resins Hemoperfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemoperfusion industry is primarily divided into two market types: charcoal hemoperfusion and resin hemoperfusion. Charcoal hemoperfusion utilizes activated charcoal as an adsorbent to effectively remove toxins and drugs from the bloodstream, making it valuable in treating acute poisoning or drug overdoses. On the other hand, certain resin hemoperfusion employs specially designed resins that selectively bind and eliminate specific toxins, providing targeted therapy for conditions like liver failure or renal dysfunction. Both approaches enhance detoxification and patient outcomes.</p></p>
