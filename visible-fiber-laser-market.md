<p><p>The Visible Fiber Laser Market is primarily segmented into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed lasers. Continuous Wave lasers emit a constant beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring stable energy output, such as materials processing and telecommunications. In contrast, Pulsed lasers release energy in short bursts, offering higher peak powers for applications like precision cutting and medical procedures. The choice between these types depends on the specific needs of the industry, including efficiency and application requirements.</p></p>
