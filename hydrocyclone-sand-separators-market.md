<p><p>Hydrocyclone sand separators are classified into vertical and horizontal types based on their orientation and application. Vertical hydrocyclones optimize space and enhance separation efficiency by using gravity to aid in the settling of particles. They are typically used in mining and wastewater treatment. In contrast, horizontal hydrocyclones provide a compact design suitable for limited spaces, offering enhanced sand removal in industrial processes. Both types effectively separate solids from liquids, catering to various industries' specific needs and operational conditions.</p></p>
