<p><p>The frozen bakery market comprises three main types: breads, ready-to-thaw, and ready-to-prove products. Frozen breads include various loaves and rolls pre-baked and quickly frozen for later use. Ready-to-thaw products are partially baked and can be quickly defrosted for immediate consumption, offering convenience without compromising freshness. Ready-to-prove items are raw dough that requires final proofing before baking, allowing for freshly baked goods at home. Together, these types cater to diverse consumer needs in convenience and quality.</p></p>
