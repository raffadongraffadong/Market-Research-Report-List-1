<p><p>The backside grinding tape market is categorized into UV type and non-UV type. UV type tapes are designed for applications requiring adhesion that can be cured or strengthened using ultraviolet light, offering superior durability and precision in the grinding process. Non-UV type tapes, on the other hand, rely on traditional adhesive properties and are suitable for standard processes without requiring UV curing. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing and other precision grinding applications, each catering to specific operational needs.</p></p>
