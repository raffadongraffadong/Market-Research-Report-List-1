<p><ul><li>Lamp-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Thermal Processing (RTP) Equipment market is divided into two primary types: lamp-based and laser-based systems. Lamp-based RTP uses high-intensity lamps to rapidly heat substrates, offering uniform temperature distribution and quick processing times, ideal for semiconductor and solar cell applications. On the other hand, laser-based RTP employs focused laser beams for precise temperature control and selective heating, providing flexibility in processing varied materials and reducing thermal damage, making it suitable for advanced microelectronics fabrication.</p></p>
