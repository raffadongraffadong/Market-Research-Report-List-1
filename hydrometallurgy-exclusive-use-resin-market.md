<p><p>The hydrometallurgy exclusive use resin market is primarily categorized into two types: gel resin and macroporous resin. Gel resin features a fine pore structure, enabling it to effectively capture specific metal ions, making it ideal for selective metal extraction processes. In contrast, macroporous resin possesses larger pore sizes, allowing for higher flow rates and greater adsorption capacities, which facilitates rapid processing of bulk metal solutions. Both types are essential for optimizing metal recovery in hydrometallurgical applications.</p></p>
