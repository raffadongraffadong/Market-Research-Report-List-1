<p><p>The bio-based polyurethane market encompasses three primary types: flexible, semi-rigid, and rigid. Flexible polyurethanes are soft and adaptable, commonly used in mattresses and upholstery. Semi-rigid polyurethanes offer a balance between flexibility and firmness, making them suitable for applications like automotive parts and insulation. Rigid polyurethanes are characterized by their tough and inflexible nature, ideal for structural components, packaging, and construction materials. All types leverage renewable resources, contributing to sustainability by reducing reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
