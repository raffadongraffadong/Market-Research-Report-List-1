<p><p>The microinjection pump market primarily comprises two types: single injection pumps and dual injection pumps. Single injection pumps are designed for precise delivery of one fluid, making them ideal for applications requiring specific dosages. In contrast, dual injection pumps can administer two different fluids simultaneously, offering enhanced versatility for complex procedures. This differentiation caters to varying needs in research and clinical settings, allowing for tailored applications in drug delivery, cell research, and bioprocessing.</p></p>
