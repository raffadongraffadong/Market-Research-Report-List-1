<p><p>The memory foam cushion market primarily consists of two types: traditional memory foam and gel memory foam. Traditional memory foam offers excellent contouring and pressure relief, adapting to body shape for optimal support. It retains heat, which can affect comfort during sleep. Gel memory foam, on the other hand, incorporates cooling gel beads or layers to enhance breathability and temperature regulation while maintaining support and pressure relief. This innovation appeals to consumers seeking comfort without the heat retention associated with traditional memory foam.</p></p>
