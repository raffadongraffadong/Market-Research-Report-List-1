<p><p>The Smart Grid IT Systems market encompasses several key technologies. Distributed Energy Resource Management Systems (DERMS) optimize the integration of decentralized energy sources. Demand Response Management Systems (DRMS) facilitate demand-side management by adjusting consumer usage during peak times. Geographic Information Systems (GIS) provide spatial analysis and mapping to enhance grid planning and maintenance. Customer Information Systems (CIS) manage customer data and interactions, ensuring efficient billing and service delivery. Together, these systems enhance grid efficiency, reliability, and consumer engagement.</p></p>
