<p><p>The Electronic Printed Circuit Board (PCB) market encompasses various types catering to different applications. Rigid 1-2 sided PCBs are simple structures for basic connectivity. Standard multilayer boards integrate multiple layers for complex circuits. HDI (High-Density Interconnect) and microvia types support compact designs and higher performance. IC substrates are tailored for advanced semiconductor packaging. Flexible circuits offer adaptability and space-saving solutions, while rigid-flex combines the benefits of both rigid and flexible technologies, enabling versatile designs in demanding environments.</p></p>
