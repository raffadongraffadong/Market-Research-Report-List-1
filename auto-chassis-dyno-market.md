<p><ul><li>Single Roller Type</li><li>Multi Roller Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto chassis dynamometer market is divided into two main types: single roller and multi roller. Single roller dynamometers feature one rolling surface, making them suitable for basic testing and lighter vehicles but may limit performance measurements. Multi roller dynamometers, on the other hand, consist of multiple rollers, enabling a more realistic simulation of real-world driving conditions. They provide better accuracy in measuring power and efficiency, catering to a broader range of vehicles, including heavier and high-performance models.</p></p>
