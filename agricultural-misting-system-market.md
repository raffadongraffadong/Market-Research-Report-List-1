<p><p>The agricultural misting system market comprises two primary types: low-pressure and high-pressure misting systems. Low-pressure misting systems operate at 10-60 PSI, ideal for cooling greenhouses and outdoor crops, ensuring gentle moisture distribution. In contrast, high-pressure misting systems function at 1000-3000 PSI, creating finer mist particles for enhanced evaporation and temperature control. These systems promote efficient water usage, reduce heat stress on plants, and optimize growth conditions, making them essential tools in modern agriculture.</p></p>
