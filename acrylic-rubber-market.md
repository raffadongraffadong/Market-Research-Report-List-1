<p><p>The acrylic rubber market comprises several types, each with unique properties. Active Chlorine Acrylate Rubber offers excellent chemical resistance and is suitable for seals and gaskets. Epoxy Acrylate Rubber is known for its superior adhesion and is commonly used in coatings and adhesives. Dienes Acrylate Rubber exhibits good mechanical properties and is often utilized in automotive applications. Carboxyl Type Acrylate Rubber enhances compatibility with fillers and improves thermal stability, making it ideal for various industrial applications.</p></p>
