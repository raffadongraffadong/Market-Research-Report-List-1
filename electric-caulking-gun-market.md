<p><p>The electric caulking gun market is divided into two main types: closed barrel and open barrel caulking guns. Closed barrel caulking guns feature a cartridge that is sealed at the back, providing a more controlled dispensing of caulk and reducing the risk of spills. In contrast, open barrel caulking guns accommodate bulk caulking materials, offering versatility for larger projects. Both types enhance efficiency and precision in application, catering to different user preferences and project requirements in construction and home improvement.</p></p>
