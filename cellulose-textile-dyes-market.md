<p><strong>The Cellulose Textile Dyes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct</li><li>Reactive</li><li>VAT</li><li>Basic</li><li>Acid</li><li>Disperse</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellulose textile dyes market is diverse, encompassing various dye types tailored for cellulose fibers. Direct dyes offer straightforward application, allowing for vivid colors but less wash fastness. Reactive dyes form chemical bonds with fibers, ensuring durability and vibrancy. VAT dyes are insoluble and require reduction for application, favored for their lightfastness. Basic dyes are cationic, suitable for blends but often less stable on cellulose. Acid dyes target protein fibers yet can also be used on modified cellulose. Disperse dyes are primarily for synthetic fibers but are increasingly used on cellulose blends, while "Others" include specialized and eco-friendly options that cater to evolving market demands.</p></p>
