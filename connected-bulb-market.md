<p><p>Connected bulb market types include ZigBee, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth, each serving distinct connectivity needs. ZigBee offers low-power, mesh networking capabilities ideal for smart homes, facilitating communication between multiple devices. Wi-Fi ensures robust internet connectivity, allowing for remote control and integration with home networks. Bluetooth, known for its short-range connection, simplifies setup and operation, suitable for personal devices. Each technology balances range, power consumption, and ease of use, catering to diverse consumer preferences and smart lighting applications.</p></p>
