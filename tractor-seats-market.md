<p><p>The tractor seats market includes **Mechanical Suspension Seats** and **Air Suspension Seats**. Mechanical seats use springs or shock absorbers to reduce vibrations, offering basic comfort at a lower cost. Air suspension seats, on the other hand, use airbags to provide superior vibration isolation and adjustable support, enhancing operator comfort during long hours. While mechanical seats are durable and cost-effective, air suspension seats are preferred for high-end tractors, prioritizing ergonomics and reducing operator fatigue in demanding conditions.</p></p>
