<p><p>Contact lens cleaner boxes come in various types, primarily categorized into vibration type, ultrasonic type, and others. </p><p>The vibration type uses mechanical vibrations to dislodge dirt and debris from lenses, ensuring effective cleaning without damaging the lenses. Ultrasonic type cleaners utilize high-frequency sound waves to create microscopic bubbles that clean lenses gently and thoroughly. Other types may include manual or chemical cleaners that rely on specific solutions or simple scrubbing methods. Each type aims to maintain lens hygiene and prolong their lifespan.</p></p>
