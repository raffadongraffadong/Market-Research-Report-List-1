<p><p>The manual variable fiber optical attenuator market consists of two main types: single mode and multimode variable fiber attenuators. Single mode variable fiber attenuators are designed for use with single mode fibers, effectively controlling signal strength over long distances, which is crucial for communication systems. On the other hand, multimode variable fiber attenuators are utilized with multimode fibers, suited for shorter distances and higher data rates. Both types serve to manage signal levels, enhancing performance in optical networks.</p></p>
