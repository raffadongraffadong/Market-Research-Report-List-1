<p><strong>The Antiviral Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors</li><li>Protease Inhibitors</li><li>Fusion Inhibitors</li><li>Immune System Modulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antiviral drugs market is categorized into several types. Reverse transcriptase inhibitors disrupt viral replication by blocking the reverse transcription process, essential for retroviruses. Protease inhibitors prevent viral protein cleavage, hindering the maturation of infectious viruses. Fusion inhibitors block the entry of viruses into host cells by interfering with the fusion process. Immune system modulators enhance the body's immune response to fight infections more effectively. Each category plays a crucial role in combating viral infections, contributing to the overall market dynamics.</p></p>
