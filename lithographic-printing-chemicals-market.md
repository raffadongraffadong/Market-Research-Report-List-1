<p><p>The Lithographic Printing Chemicals market comprises various types essential for the printing process. **Inks** are pigmented liquids used to transfer images onto substrates, offering durability and color precision. **Fountain Solutions** maintain the non-printing areas of plates by balancing ink and water, ensuring print clarity. **Cleaning Solutions** remove ink residues and debris from equipment, maintaining operational efficiency. **Others** include additives, coatings, and solvents that enhance print quality, adhesion, and drying.
