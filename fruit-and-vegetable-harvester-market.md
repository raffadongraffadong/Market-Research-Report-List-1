<p><p>The fruit and vegetable harvester market is categorized into three main types: self-propelled harvesters, trailed harvesters, and others. Self-propelled harvesters are independent machines that can navigate fields on their own, offering greater maneuverability and efficiency. Trailed harvesters are attached to a tractor, allowing for flexibility and ease of use across various terrains. The "others" category encompasses specialized or hybrid machines that combine features of both types or are designed for specific crops, enhancing versatility in harvesting operations.</p></p>
