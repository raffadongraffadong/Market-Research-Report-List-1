<p><ul><li>Water-based Polyurethane Compounds</li><li>Solvent-based Polyurethane Compounds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane solution market is categorized into water-based and solvent-based polyurethane compounds. Water-based polyurethane compounds are environmentally friendly options that utilize water as a solvent, making them less toxic and reducing volatile organic compound emissions. They are commonly used in coatings, adhesives, and sealants. In contrast, solvent-based polyurethane compounds use organic solvents, offering enhanced durability and performance for heavy-duty applications. These compounds are widely applied in industries such as automotive, furniture, and construction due to their resilience and adhesive strength.</p></p>
