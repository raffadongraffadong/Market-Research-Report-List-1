<p><strong>The OLED Fine Metal Mask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Etching</li><li>Electroforming</li><li>Composite Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED Fine Metal Mask market is divided into three main types: etching, electroforming, and composite methods. The etching process involves creating intricate patterns on the metal mask using chemical or mechanical means, allowing for precise pixel alignment. Electroforming utilizes an electrochemical process to deposit metal onto a mold, ensuring a high-quality, flexible mask. The composite method combines different materials to enhance durability and efficiency. Each method offers unique advantages, catering to diverse manufacturing requirements in OLED display production.</p></p>
