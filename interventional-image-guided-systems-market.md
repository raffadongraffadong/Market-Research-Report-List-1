<p><p>Interventional image-guided systems encompass various technologies used for minimally invasive procedures. Interventional CT image-guided systems utilize computed tomography for precise targeting during biopsies and treatments. Interventional X-ray systems provide real-time imaging for vascular procedures and catheter placements. MRI image-guided systems offer high-resolution imaging for soft tissue evaluation, while interventional ultrasound systems assist in guiding needles and other instruments with real-time visuals. Other market categories include hybrid systems and emerging technologies that combine multiple imaging modalities for enhanced accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
