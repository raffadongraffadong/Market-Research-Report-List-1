<p><ul><li>Water-based Label Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based Label Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The label adhesive market is primarily divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based label adhesives use water as a solvent, offering a more environmentally friendly option with low VOC emissions, making them ideal for applications requiring safe handling. Solvent-based label adhesives, on the other hand, utilize organic solvents, providing strong adhesion and durability, suitable for demanding environments. Each type caters to different industry needs, balancing sustainability and performance based on application requirements.</p></p>
