<p><strong>The Electrical Test Pencil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type Test Pencil</li><li>Induction Type Test Pencil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical test pencil market includes two primary types: contact type and induction type test pencils. </p><p>Contact type test pencils require direct contact with an electrical conductor to detect voltage, offering precise readings. They are commonly used for close-range testing in electrical maintenance. </p><p>In contrast, induction type test pencils detect voltage from a distance by sensing the electromagnetic field around live wires, providing non-contact voltage detection. This makes them ideal for safety applications and for use in tight or hazardous spaces.</p></p>
