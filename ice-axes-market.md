<p><p>Ice axes are specialized tools used in mountaineering, climbing, and glacier travel, and they come in two primary market types: bent and straight axes. Bent axes feature a curved shaft that enhances ergonomics and allows for better hand positioning, making them suitable for difficult vertical climbs. In contrast, straight axes offer a traditional design that provides stability and versatility for general ice climbing and glacier travel. The choice between these types typically depends on the specific climbing conditions and personal preferences of the user.</p></p>
